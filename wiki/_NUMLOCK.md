The [_NUMLOCK](_NUMLOCK) statement sets the state of the Num Lock key.

## Syntax

> [_NUMLOCK](_NUMLOCK) {ON|OFF|_TOGGLE}

## Availability

* Version 1.4 and up.
* [Keywords currently not supported](Keywords-currently-not-supported-by-QB64).
